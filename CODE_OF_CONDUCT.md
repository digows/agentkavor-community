# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Discuss ideas, evidence, and trade-offs rather than attacking people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish confidential information.
- Search for existing conversations and avoid spam or repeated promotion.
- Accept moderation decisions made to protect the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized content, sustained disruption, impersonation, and publishing another person's private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior violates this Code of Conduct.

Report conduct concerns privately to [contact@agentkavor.com](mailto:contact@agentkavor.com). Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

This Code of Conduct applies in all Kavor community spaces and when someone officially represents the project.
